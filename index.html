FAQ

Why Sudoflex?

The name 'Sudoflex' embodies our flexible approach to solving sudoku puzzles using OCR technology.

What is OCR in Sudoku?

OCR is a technology that allows you to recognize characters in an image. In our case, we use it to recognize numbers in the Sudoku grid.
